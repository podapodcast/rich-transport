Driver Opportunities

Join Our Team of Professional Drivers
At Rich Logistics Transports LLC, we’re always looking for motivated, reliable, and dedicated drivers to join our growing team. Whether you’re an experienced courier or just starting your journey in the logistics industry, we provide the tools and support you need to succeed. As part of our driver team, you'll have the opportunity to earn a competitive income while enjoying flexible schedules and the chance to grow your career.

Why Drive with Us?

  • Competitive Pay: Earn a steady income with opportunities for bonuses and incentives based on performance.
  • Flexible Scheduling: We understand that life can be busy. That's why we offer flexible hours that fit your schedule.
  • Local & Regional Routes: Enjoy consistent work with routes that allow you to be home at the end of the day.
  • Competitive Bonuses: Depending on the contract, drivers can qualify for gas cards, equipment provisions (such as GPS devices or vehicle maintenance support), and other performance-based bonuses.
  • Growth Opportunities: As our company grows, so do your opportunities. Advance your career with roles in management, dispatch, and more.

Requirements to Apply:

  • Valid driver’s license
  • Clean driving record
  • Reliable vehicle
  • Willingness to comply with safety protocols and industry regulations
  • Strong communication skills
  • BBP & HIPPA Certified
  • Experience in medical or general courier services (preferred but not required)
